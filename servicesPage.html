Electric Trucks

Our electric trucks represent the pinnacle of zero-emission transportation technology. They are ideal for urban and short-haul routes, where they deliver exceptional performance and zero tailpipe emissions. These trucks are equipped with advanced battery systems that provide long-range capabilities and fast charging options, ensuring that delivery schedules are maintained without compromising on environmental standards.

Hybrid Trucks

For operations requiring greater flexibility and extended range, our hybrid trucks offer a blend of traditional and alternative fuel technologies. By combining an internal combustion engine with electric propulsion, these trucks achieve superior fuel efficiency and lower emissions compared to conventional vehicles. This hybrid approach is particularly effective for long-haul routes where the electric range may not be sufficient alone but where significant fuel savings and emissions reductions are still beneficial.

Biodiesel Trucks

Our biodiesel trucks use fuel derived from renewable biological sources, such as vegetable oils and animal fats. Biodiesel is a cleaner alternative to petroleum diesel, reducing carbon emissions and reliance on fossil fuels. These trucks are well-suited for clients who prefer to use existing diesel infrastructure while still contributing to sustainability goals.
